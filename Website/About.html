Prime HRM

The Prime Heart Rate Monitor is a project done for GT block about designing and
creating our own piece of werable assessment technology.
The project took many weeks to prepare.


Our heart rate monitor uses a sensor to measure your heart rate in real-time.
The data is then displayed on the monitor screen,
allowing you to see your heart rate with a quick glance. The monitor also
gives information on how to increase or decrease heart rate.
