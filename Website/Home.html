Welcome to our heart rate monitor website!

We needed a way to make sure the heart rate monitor was able to detect human
pulse for when someone puts there hand on the scanner we
thought of various different way and ended up reverse engineering a finger pulse monitor
and took out some of the components in the monitor and added them into our product after various different attempts were made we finally were able to make a heart
rate monitor accurate enough to scan human pulse.
